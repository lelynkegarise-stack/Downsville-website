Welcome to the Downsville Ruritan Club!

Serving Downsville Maryland and the surronding area since 1954 Ruritans purpose is to create a better understanding among people and making Americas communities bettwe through volunteer comunity service Ruritans slogan is "fellowship, goodwill and community service." Our members, along with members around the country represent their commnuity in which the club serves. People of all occupations, social postions, and backgrounds are encouraged to join the Ruritan
